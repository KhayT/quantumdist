Blockchain Technology Training


Blockchain is a hot topic within the industry right now as more organizations are using the technology to process cryptocurrency payments between different parties.


This 'Introduction to Blockchain' course has been designed to explain what the technology is and how it works at a high level You will build an awareness of Blockchain technology and how it can be used to process cryptocurrency transactions across an open and distributes ledger.


Completion of this course will ensure you are able to determine how Blockchain can support your business operations.
